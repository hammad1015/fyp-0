How to Use MailEx?

Want to use MailEx, but don't know where to start?

Don't worry. Follow these simple steps to get started:

  1. Register on our Website. Here.
  2. Once registered, login to the website using your registered email and password.
  3. Enable automatic forwarding of your emails to the MailEx email : MailEx@gmail.com
  4. Select the time and you can chose if you want to download or preview the processed email
